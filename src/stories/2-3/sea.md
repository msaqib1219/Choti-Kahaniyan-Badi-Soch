# Title: The Lost Baby Turtle
Level: 2-3
Moral: Always help a friend in need.

## Scene 1
Text: A tiny baby turtle is on the sandy beach of Karachi. He wants to go home.
Action Instruction: Tap the turtle to help it move
Action Type: tap
Action Target: Baby Turtle
Action Emoji: 🐢
