# Title: The Thirsty Sparrow
Level: 2-3
Moral: Helping others feels good!

## Scene 1
Text: A little sparrow is near a big Banyan tree. She is very thirsty.
Action Instruction: Tap the sparrow to help her fly
Action Type: tap
Action Target: Little Sparrow
Image Prompt: A cute little sparrow bird sitting on a branch of a large Banyan tree, lush green forest, soft sunlight, 3D cartoon style

## Scene 2
Text: Zoya brings a small clay bowl (kundi) with cool water.
Action Instruction: Tap the water to fill the bowl
Action Type: multi-tap
Action Count: 3
Action Target: Water Bowl
Image Prompt: A Pakistani girl in traditional clothes putting a small water bowl near a tree, friendly bird watching, soft watercolor style

## Scene 3
Text: The sparrow drinks the water. Chirp chirp! 'Thank you, Zoya!'
Action Instruction: Tap the sparrow to hear her sing
Action Type: tap
Action Target: Happy Bird
Image Prompt: Happy sparrow drinking from a clay bowl, colorful flowers around, Zoya smiling in background, bright and cheerful
